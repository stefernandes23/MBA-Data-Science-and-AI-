# 🎓 MBA - Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

This repository contains projects and activities developed during my **MBA in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence**. It serves as a personal reference and a way to share knowledge with the community. Throughout the course, I explored various areas of Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI, ranging from business strategies to advanced data modeling and machine learning techniques.

## 🛠️ **Skills & Tools Gained**
During the MBA, I developed a broad range of skills across multiple domains of data science and AI:

- **Machine Learning & Deep Learning**: Building predictive models, applying generative AI techniques to address real-world business challenges.
- **Hypothesis Testing, Regression & Clustering**: Using statistical models to infer relationships in data and make informed business decisions.
- **Advanced Machine Learning Techniques**: Application of deep learning models such as convolutional and recurrent neural networks, transformers, and reinforcement learning systems.
- **AI Business Strategies**: Understanding how data science and AI are applied across industries, optimizing processes, and improving decision-making capabilities.

## 🚀 **Projects Highlight**
Here are some of the projects I've completed during my MBA:

1. **Descriptive Data Analysis and Linear Regression Models**  
   - Conducted a descriptive analysis and applied linear regression models to various datasets:
     - Car insurance claims data.
     - Case-control study from the Anatomy and Pathology Department of a hospital in São Paulo.
     - Linear regression to study life expectancy.

2. **Predictive Model for Credit Scoring**  
   - Developed a predictive credit scoring model, analyzing factors influencing credit scores and building a machine learning model to predict scores.

3. **SmartMind Analytics - Data Architecture**  
   - Designed and implemented a comprehensive data architecture for the SmartMind Analytics project, including data integration and management strategies.

4. **Multivariate Linear Regression - House Price Prediction**  
   - Built a multivariate linear regression model to predict house prices based on various features such as number of rooms, house size, and location.

- **AI & NLP APIs**: ChatGPT, Bard/PaLM, Langchain
